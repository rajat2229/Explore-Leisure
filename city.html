Top Ten Sites For
    City Break

Want a city break but don't know where?
No worries! We got your back. Here are the top ten trending places for adventure lovers.
This will give you an idea on where you should travel to....

Udaipur

A royal city with magnificent forts, palaces and innumerable man-made lakes, Udaipur is a perfect weekend getaway from Delhi to chill and relax. You can enjoy watching sunsets from the forts or palaces, going on boat rides on the vast lakes, exploring the opulent City Palace or Vintage Car Museum and relishing some authentic Rajasthani street food in Udaipur, never actually wanting to head back home! Such is the charm and magic of this city.

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, which means Hill Fort of Jaisal, majestically stands on the ridge of golden yellow sandstone housing magnificent forts, lakes, and palaces, and is another exciting weekend getaway from Delhi. Shimmering in the golden hue owing to the yellow sandstone used in building forts and the town itself, Jaisalmer is rightly called the Golden City of India.

Jodhpur

Need an awesome destination from Delhi for an impromptu and a quick weekend trip? Try Jodhpur for a change and thank us later! The Blue City is as charming and magical as other cities in Rajasthan and offers you a number of options to spend your weekend here.

Rishikesh

One of the most sought-after weekend getaways from Delhi, Rishikesh is the destination where you can enjoy the best of both worlds – try adrenaline-pumping adventure sports or soak in the tranquillity and serenity visiting the various temples here. Whatever you choose, Rishikesh will ensure to keep you engaged and entertained!

Satara

Up for exploring a city seeped in history for a weekend getaway? Well, Satara is your place to go. Apart from the rich history, Satara is also famous for the seven hills surrounding it (which is why it is named Satara), namely Sajjangad, Ajinkyatara, Jarandeshwar, Yawateshwar, Kitlichadongar, Nakdichadongar, and PedhyachaBhairoba. A perfect family weekend getaway from Mumbai, Satara offers you everything from museums, temples, lakes, falls to forts and incredible viewpoints.

Mahabaleshwar

Land of mystical hills, fresh strawberries, lush greenery and picturesque surroundings is how one would describe Mahabaleshwar. Undoubtedly one of the most amazing weekend getaways from Mumbai, Mahabaleshwar is as attractive for romantic getaways for couples as it is for family trips.

Lonavala

Lonavala provides you with the respite you need from fast-paced and busy cities like Mumbai and Pune. beautiful landscape, amazing weather, serenity and lush greenery of Lonavala attracts couples who plan a quick romantic weekend trip here or even families that wish to enjoy a short vacation in someplace away from the chaos of the city. You blend with nature and its incredible beauty while holidaying here and are transported to a world with less chaos and more peace!

Madurai

A charismatic city of temples in Tamil Nadu, Madurai is a quick weekend getaway from Chennai and an astounding destination to enjoy solace and peace in the innumerable temples dotting the city. Soaked in art and architecture evident in the various architectural wonders here, this city is perfect for a nice and short family vacation or for those who love to backpack to historic cities. Witness the vibrant culture of Tamilians, enjoy mouthfuls of delicious South Indian food and take a tour of this temple city as much as you like.

Pondicherry

A laidback beach paradise of South India, Pondicherry is indeed an amazing girls weekend getaway from Chennai. Located just a 3-hour drive away from Chennai, this small yet incredible destination is a favourite among youngsters, couples and old people. From peaceful and tranquil ashrams, beautiful temples, churches to gorgeous beaches, Pondicherry offers everything you need to take a break from the city life and enjoy some much-needed “me time”.

Varanasi

The holy city of Varanasi houses one of the Jyotirlingas in India and is renowned for its culture, silk and muslin fabric, sculpture, ivory works and perfumes. An excellent weekend getaway from Kolkata in case you wish to enjoy a long drive, Varanasi is a must-visit destination for all history buffs and temple lovers. Take a dip in the holy waters of Ganga, enjoy the scintillating evening aarti and visit the numerous temples and ghats in the city for a truly spiritual experience.
